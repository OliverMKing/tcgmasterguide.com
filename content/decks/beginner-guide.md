# Getting Started

Welcome to the world of Pokemon Trading Card Game! This guide will help you understand the basics and get you started on your TCG journey.

## Basic Card Types

There are three main types of cards in Pokemon TCG:

- **Pokemon Cards** - The creatures you use to battle
- **Trainer Cards** - Support cards that provide various effects
- **Energy Cards** - Required to power your Pokemon's attacks

## How to Play

Each player starts with a deck of 60 cards. You draw 7 cards to start, and must have at least one Basic Pokemon to begin. The goal is to knock out your opponent's Pokemon and take all 6 of your Prize cards.

## Building Your First Deck

Start with a theme deck or build around a Pokemon you like. A good beginner deck typically includes:

- 15-20 Pokemon cards
- 25-30 Trainer cards
- 12-15 Energy cards

## Key Strategies for Beginners

1. **Focus on consistency** - Include multiple copies of key cards
2. **Balance your energy** - Make sure you have enough energy for your attackers
3. **Include draw support** - Cards that let you draw more cards are essential
4. **Practice, practice, practice** - The more you play, the better you'll understand the game
