# Top Tier Decks

The current competitive meta is diverse with several strong archetypes competing for the top spots.

## Dominant Strategies

### Charizard ex

High damage output with energy acceleration. This deck focuses on quickly powering up Charizard ex to deal massive damage while maintaining board control.

**Strengths:**
- Consistent high damage
- Good prize trading
- Strong early game pressure

**Weaknesses:**
- Vulnerable to disruption
- Requires setup time

### Gardevoir ex

Control deck with energy manipulation. Gardevoir ex excels at controlling the pace of the game while building up a powerful board state.

**Strengths:**
- Energy acceleration
- Versatile attackers
- Good recovery

**Weaknesses:**
- Slow setup
- Weak to aggressive decks

### Lost Zone Variants

Utilizing the Lost Zone mechanic for powerful effects. These decks trade resources for explosive plays and unique win conditions.

**Strengths:**
- Unique mechanics
- Powerful late game
- Difficult to counter

**Weaknesses:**
- Resource intensive
- Complex to pilot

## Counter Strategies

Understanding the meta allows you to tech your deck appropriately:

- **Path to the Peak** - Counters ability-dependent decks
- **Boss's Orders** - Controls opponent's active Pokemon
- **Counter Catcher** - Punishes opponents who take early prizes
- **Energy disruption** - Slows down energy-reliant strategies

## Meta Trends

The meta shifts with new set releases and tournament results. Stay informed by:
- Following major tournament results
- Testing against popular decks
- Adapting your deck to counter prevalent strategies
