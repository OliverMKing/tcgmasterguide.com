## Welcome to TCG Master Guide

Thanks for visiting! TCG Master Guide is your go-to resource for in-depth Pokemon TCG deck guides, written and constantly updated by Grant Manley.

## Browsing Deck Guides

From the [homepage](/), you'll see all available deck guides organized by tier. Simply click on any deck to open its guide, which includes decklists, strategy breakdowns, and matchup analysis. 

## Subscribing for Full Access

Each deck guide includes a free preview, but the full content - detailed strategy, matchup analysis, and video content - is available to subscribers. Head over to the [Subscribe](/subscribe) page to get full access to every guide on the site. You can cancel anytime from the same page.

### Questions and Answers for Subscribers

Subscribers also get access to the [Q&A](/qa) page, where you can ask anything you want about the Pokemon TCG. Whether it's a specific matchup question, a deck building decision, or general strategy advice, Q&A is there to help.

### Staying Up to Date

Grant is constantly updating deck guides to keep up with the evolving meta. Here's how to stay on top of changes:

- **Last updated date** - Every deck guide shows a "Last updated" date near the top of the page. Check this to see how recently the guide was refreshed.
- **View History** - Subscribers can click the **View History** button at the top of any deck guide to see exactly what changed in each update. This makes it easy to catch up on new tech options, revised matchup advice, or updated decklists without re-reading the entire guide.

We hope you enjoy the site and find it a valuable resource for your competitive Pokemon TCG journey!
