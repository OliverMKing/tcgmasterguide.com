# Product Overview

## Mission
Provide comprehensive, expert-level guides for the Competitive Pokemon Trading Card Game (TCG) metagame.

## Target Audience
Competitive Pokemon TCG players seeking detailed deck guides, matchup analysis, and strategic insights.

## Core Features

### 1. Landing Page
- Introduction to the website and its offerings
- Clear value proposition for competitive players
- Overview of available content and subscription benefits

### 2. Deck Guides
Comprehensive guides for current metagame decks including:
- **Deck Lists**: Complete card lists with counts
- **Matchup Analysis**: Detailed breakdowns of matchups against other meta decks
- **Play Guides**: Strategic advice on how to pilot the deck and play specific matchups
- **Recommendations**: Tech choices, alternative cards, and optimization suggestions

### 3. Navigation & Organization
- **Appendix**: Central hub linking to all available deck guides and pages
- Easy navigation between different deck guides

### 4. Subscription & Access Control
- **Paywall**: Content locked behind authentication
- **Monthly Subscription**: Recurring payment model for access
- User account creation and management
- Secure payment processing
- Subscription status tracking

## Revenue Model
Monthly subscription-based access to premium competitive Pokemon TCG content.
